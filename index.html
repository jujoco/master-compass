Master Compass

This interactive circle packing visualization displays the hierarchical structure of the company organization. Each circle represents a node in the hierarchy.

Views Available:

  • Client > Products > Features > Technology > Owner
  • Region > Domain > Team

How to navigate:

  • Switch between views using the tabs at the top
  • Click on any circle to zoom into that section
  • Click on the background to zoom back out to the root
  • Click on nodes in the tree below to navigate
